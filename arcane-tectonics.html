Arcane Tectonics

Programmers: Brandon Walker, Tyler Thompson, Alejandro DeVore, Joseph Ienna
Designers: Annie Hughey, Rowena Chen

In the spring of 2018 I was a UX designer for the award winning mobile game, Arcane Tectonics, in the advanced game design course at Cornell University. Arcane Tectonics is a match-3 puzzle based combat game, in which you must protect Mika from evil monsters by using her tectonic shifting powers in order to make matches of 3 or more of the same type beneath the monster's feet.

Arcane Tectonics was accepted into the first round of Boston FIG, an indie game competition.

Group Members: Annie, Tyler, Alejandro, Tyler, Brandon, Joe, Rowena
