Introductory Programming & Design for the Web

Course Syllabus

This semester I am a teaching assistant for the introductory web programming course (INFO 1300) at Cornell. As a TA I am responsible for teaching a weekly section with another TA to 20 students, holding weekly office hours, and attending weekly TA meetings & grading sessions. TAs are responsible for grading the same 10 students throughout the semester and giving them personalized, constructive feedback on their websites' implementation and design.

This course covers the basics of web programming, teaching HTML, CSS, JavaScript, and PHP. It also addresses visual, interaction, and usability principles in designing webpages.I am a strong believer that anyone can learn to code, all you need is a positive learning environment and the will to fail. When I first learned to program I felt overwhelmed, confused, and scared to ask a stupid question. I work hard to make sure my students feel comfortable and safe in learning environments, while asking thought provoking questions.
