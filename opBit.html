Operation Bitwise

Programmers: Brandon Walker, Tyler Thompson, Alejandro DeVore, David Ma
Designers: Annie Hughey, Sophia Deng

In the spring of 2017 I was a UX designer for the award winning game, Operation Bitwise, in the introductory game design course at Cornell University. Intro game design is a rigorous five credit course teaching programmers and designers how to work together to develop usable, fun games and software. The course is structured by teams of six, four programmers and two designers, that are instructed to meet specific functional requirements throughout the semester. Teams present their progress every two weeks, and are required to write weekly technical documents explaining the game's mechanics and design in detail.

Game design has been the most challenging and rewarding course I've taken at Cornell, and here I'll describe in detail the evolution of Operation Bitwise. This page will describe and show the evolution and steps involved in the design and development of OpBit.
