Mambo! I'm Annie, a senior at Cornell University pursuing a bachelor's degree in Information Science.

I am a young developer & designer who's passionate about making the world a kinder place through intelligent, inspired design.

Spokane, Washington is my home. With Ithaca, New York a close second. This past summer I enjoyed exploring and living in the Gold Coast of Chicago, working for a web development firm.

This site is owned by myself, with the use of our friend Bootstrap :-)
Here's a picture of me from last spring! "Incroyable!"

Design

One of my major concentrations is in User Experience. I have been trained in and have experience with HCI and UX design and research. I am currently minoring in Game Design and was a designer for the award winning slide-until-blocked puzzle game, Operation Bitwise at the Game Design Initiative at Cornell's showcase in May 2017. I also am a research assitant in the Virtual Embodiement Lab at Cornell University where I design models in Unity for human-subject experiments in VR using the Oculus Rift and the HTC Vive. This semester I am on the Lunar Phases team, a physics education research project funded by Oculus Rift.

Develop

My other concentration is in Interactive Technologies. I have experience with Object Oriented Programming & Data Structures as well as Web Programming including the development of this site. I am proficient in the programming languages: Java, Python, MatLab, Arduino, C#, HTML, CSS, PHP, MySQL, JavaScript, jQuery,and have used Bootstrap and other development frameworks. I have experience with Linux/UNIXoperating systems and bash scripting. I am currently in a Rapid Prototyping & Physical Computing course in which my team is designing and building a theremin contolled light display.

Dialogue

I also have studied Swahili for three years at Cornell, and have a large interest in the study of the many forms of discrimination and oppression in the United States. I am a registered Citizen Potawatomi Nationtribal member, and my family and I are semi-active members. I am currently participating in the Intergroup Dialogue Project and consider myself an ally to oppressed communities. I am passionate about educating myself about the lives of others in order to become a better contributor to the world and to the people around me. I believe we have strength in our differences.
